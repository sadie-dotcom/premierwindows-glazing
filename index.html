Before you enquire

Double glazing Perth FAQs

How much does uPVC double glazing cost in Perth?
Average cost is $1,300–$3,000 per window supplied and installed; a typical 3-bedroom home runs $8,000–$15,000. Every home is custom, so your quote is exact and free.
How long does installation take?
Most homes are fitted in 1–4 days on site by our own trained teams — never subcontractors — with minimal disruption. As a guide, plan for around 10–14 weeks from order to installation while your windows are custom-made in Balcatta.
Does double glazing improve home security?
Yes. Every Premier frame has multi-point locking and reinforced steel cores as standard, and our double glazed units meet AS 2047 — significantly harder to force than old single glazed aluminium.
What energy ratings do your windows meet?
Our Low-E, argon-filled double glazed units reach U-values as low as 1.55 W/m²K (7-star NatHERS) and comply with AS 2047 and AS 1288 — cutting heating and cooling costs by up to 40%.
What about condensation or maintenance?
uPVC never rots, corrodes or needs repainting — just wipe the frames clean. Double glazing also helps reduce the condensation that forms on cold single glazed aluminium frames.
Do you make and install yourselves?
Yes — we manufacture every window and door in our own Balcatta factory and install with our own trained WA teams, never subcontractors. That's how we control quality from consultation to aftercare, backed by a 10-year warranty.
